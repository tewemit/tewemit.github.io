About Tewelle

Tewelle is a Senior Software Engineer who enjoys working in international and multicultural environments where innovation and challenges open opportunities for developing exciting projects and experiences. He brings requirements and challenges to life through best-practice solutions complying with the underlying architectures and business models.

Tewelle endeavors to enhance the product suite through pushing technology boundaries and providing innovative solutions. He motivates his endeavor by ensuring Software Engineers in his project/product team are fully supported and have the required tools to improve and support the delivery of that product. Beyond supporting developers and recommending areas for improvement, Tewelle also cares about accessibility, cross-platform compatibility, scalability, code quality and system documentation.

From a research & development point of view, Tewelle is interested and knowledgeable in the areas of e-health, data mining and machine learning. Apart from that, Tewelle is an enthusiast in technology, innovation and entrepreneurship.
